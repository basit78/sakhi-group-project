About Us

Sakhi Group of companies was established in 2002 as a local brokerage house. The small plant sowed as a seed in 2002 by the Group Owner and Managing Director Dileep Kumar and His Partner Dilip Kumar, has today grown out to be a tree with fruitful branches providing our services in the field of Imports, Exports, Trade, Indent, Local Brokerage, Production, Warehousing and Supply Chain. A team of Young dedicated professional full of enthusiasm and love of pulse industry has made Sakhi Group one of the most trusted and reliable trade partners for our clients across the globe.

Sakhi Group of Companies along with their vast experience in the field of Pulses and Grains, also has a very strong, trustworthy, loyal and reliable client network of suppliers, buyers, traders, stockiest, manufacturers and distributors. Being one of the reputed names of the industry today our clients believe in the quality of our work and the faithfulness in our words.


Our Moto is “SAKHI, The Name of Trust” and we assure our clients to trust us with their requirements and needs for all the pulses related trade and business. The group today has clients across the globe and the Sakhi Community keeps growing with the passage of time

The Group

SAKHI INTERNATIONAL

We specialize in all kinds of Pulses, Food – Feed Grains and Seeds. Sakhi International is a well renowned name in the Pulse Industry of Pakistan. Sakhi International was established in 2002 in Karachi. Today Sakhi International is one of the biggest names in the market locally as well as Internationally for Desi Chickpeas Trade in Pakistan. Sakhi International is also a leading Exporter of Pakistan Origin Sesame Seeds. The major trade partners for our exports include China, India, Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand, Taiwan, Malaysia and most of the Far East Region. We also export Fenugreek Seeds, Fennel Seeds, Chia Seeds, Wheat, Wheat Flour and other by products like Suji and Maida. Recently we’ve included Fresh Onion and Potatoes to our portfolio considering the huge demand from our clients. Sakhi International today is one of the biggest entities in the pulse industry holding a reputable market credibility. With the passage of time the number of clients also have increased significantly. Considering the demands and requirements a few other entities were introduced in time to facilitate and support our clients in their demands regarding their Grain Trade requirements.


AROON COMMODITIES

Aroon Commodities Started the Business In The Year 2009 As An Importer and Exporter for Pulses and Grains. The Company Started Operations from Karachi. The ability to handle bulk cargo in big quantities in quick time gives Aroon Commodities an advantage over its competitors in the market.


TESHA ENTERPRISE

The Third Branch of The Sakhi Group Of Companies Is Tesha Enterprise. Tesha Enterprise is a leading agro commodity Indenting (Brokerage) House based in Karachi, Pakistan. Our Vast Global Network Ensures that our customers receive the most competitive prices from the Industry’s Most Reliable and Reputable Suppliers. We specialize in Pulses, Food-Feed Grains, Oilseeds, Wheat, Rice, Sugar, Yellow Maize and Spices. The company was established in 2008 to facilitate the huge client base of Sakhi Group of Companies with their International Purchases and Requirements To and From Pakistan. Currently we are doing business with Australia, Canada, USA, Argentine, Mexico, Brazil, Russia, Ukraine, Turkey, China, India, UAE, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Taiwan, Vietnam, Thailand, Kenya, Ethiopia and Sudan. The Team of Young Professionals with their passion and love of Pulses ensures the best customer service to our clients all across the globe.
